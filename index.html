Cane Cut Collective

Cane Cut Collective is a private, invite only community of rum enthusiasts across the United States.

Our purpose is to bring together people passionate about rum (other sugarcane spirits included) and make it possible to experience sought after, rare, expensive and/or hard to acquire rums in an accessible and affordable way.

We welcome any rum enthusiasts interested in rum samples, bottle splits or sample swaps to join our community.

To receive an invitation, inquire through an existing member.
